Vincent van Gogh

A misunderstood genius. -Since 30 March 1853 ~ 29 July 1890-

-The Starry Night- The one of the world-renowned paintings.Nowadays the painting is located in Museum of Modern Art, New York City


Here's a time line of Vincent van Gogh's life:

  • 1853 - Vincent Willem van Gogh was born in Groot-Zundert
  • 1860~1880 - Decide to become an artist
  • 1881~1882 - Return to Etten for an extended stay with his parents, and he continued to draw, often using his neighbours as subjects
  • 1883~1886 - In Nuenen, Van Gogh focused on painting and drawing, he completed sketches and paintings of weavers and their cottages.
  • 1886 - Start to attend drawing classes after plaster models at the Antwerp Academy
  • 1886~1888 - Move to Paris where he shared Theo's rue Laval apartment in Montmartre and studied at Fernand Cormon's studio.

    In Antwerp he had become interested in Japanese ukiyo-e woodblock prints and had used them to decorate the walls of his studio
  • 1888 - Suffering from smoker's cough,he sought refuge in Arles. Gauguin visit Arles, Van Gogh hoped for friendship and the realisation of his idea of an artists' collective.

    After the altercation with Gauguin,Van Gogh returned to his room, where he severed his left ear with a razor causing severe bleeding.
  • 1889 - Enter the Saint-Paul-de-Mausole asylum , accompanied by his caregiver,Frédéric Salles, a clergyman.
  • 1890 - Being included in the sixth exhibition of the Société des Artistes Indépendants in the Pavillon de la Ville de Paris on the Champs-Elysées.
    Claude Monet said that his work was the best in the show.
  • 1890 - Van Gogh is believed to have shot himself in the chest with a 7mm Lefaucheux pinfire revolver.
    There were no witnesses and he died 30 hours after the incident,aged 37.
